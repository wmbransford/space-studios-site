Phase 2 — how we fix it

01 / Targeting

Lookalike off your existing clients

We stop advertising to cold interest-based audiences and start advertising to people who look like your actual book. Fewer, better-matched impressions.

02 / CTA

Direct call booking, not lead forms

The ad's job is to put a booked call on your calendar — not to generate a lead you have to chase. Zero follow-up lag.

03 / Commitment filter

Friction at the door

We'll test small-dollar booking fees, intake questions, and other filters to screen for real intent before a prospect hits your calendar. Exact mechanic gets tuned as data comes in.
